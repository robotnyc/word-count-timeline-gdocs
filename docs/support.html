Common Issues

1. Not all revisions have word counts

This is a limitation of the Google Drive™ API. Depending on the hot-cache on Google's backend, only some unnamed revisions are returned. The add-on will attempt 3 times to load more revisions to get a more complete picture.

Solution: You can also trigger this manually by clicking the Timeline Refresh button within the add-on sidebar.
