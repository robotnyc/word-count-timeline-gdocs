Word Count Timeline Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 21, 2026

Word Count Timeline ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use the Word Count Timeline Google Docs add-on.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information. The add-on only accesses the word count and timestamp of your active Google Doc to generate a timeline. This data is extracted locally within your browser session.

2. Data Storage

All word count data and timeline history are stored exclusively within the Document Properties of the specific Google Doc where the add-on is used. We do not maintain any external databases or servers to store your document content or metrics.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any of your data to third parties. Since all data stays within your Google account and the document itself, we never have access to your private information.

4. Google User Data

The add-on's use of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

5. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository.
